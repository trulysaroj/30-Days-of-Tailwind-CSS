About FoodMario

Foodmario is Nepal’s first and only platform that connects home chefs with customers all over Kathmandu Valley. The chefs who use Foodmario’s services have been thoroughly vetted by our exacting team for hygiene, quality, taste and consistency. Ordering healthy, home-cooked fare from Foodmario chefs is as easy as scrolling through our app, clicking on the fare of your choice and proceeding to check out.

As of early 2019, we have connected hundreds of home chefs with customers and delivered more than 100,000 orders. And by the end of 2019, we hope to work with more than 100 home chefs. We also envision replicating the Foodmario model in major cities across Nepal & other countries.
